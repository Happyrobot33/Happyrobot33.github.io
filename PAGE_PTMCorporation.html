Working at PTM Corporation

At PTM Corporation, I created weld fixtures and FANUC Robot Programs. Solidworks and FANUC ROBOGUIDE were the two programs I used while working here

Weld Fixtures

My main job at PTM Corporation was designing weld fixtures for various car parts. The parts were from almost every major car manufacturer, including Tesla.

FANUC Programs

I virtually programmed FANUC robots in ROBOGUIDE to weld parts, to ensure we could reach all of the welds on the fixture. I was the sole person doing this job, with the only other person able to program the robots being n the shop working on the physical robot cell
